F-E-ciency

June 10, 2014 by violkhor

My previous manager is an advocate for efficiency. Dan will not hesitate to present a business case to upgrade the hardware/software where needed. Having used Eclipse for a while, I do like the auto-formatting capabilities that is present on the IDE. The time spent installing the git plugin makes coding more comfortable on the GUI friendly Windows machine while providing seamless integration for deployment on the Amazon EC2 Server.

Oh, on the topic of AWS, I just found out that their DynamoDb is accessible via the free tier. Finally get to swap out the time designing relations in favor of - no relations? Adding Cassandra, Hadoop, MongoDb to my language list might impress my girlfriend.
